FROM CLULESS TO PROGRAMMER

Throughout my 6 years of Secondary School, I had no interest in Computer Science. My knowledge of Technology began in my final year when I joined the CISCO Computer Club. I can recall coding fun and interactive stories with my friends using SCRATCH
Months after joining the club, I was selected to attend a CISCO Networking Conference. There, I was introduced to 3D printing, Computer Networks, and the tech world in general. I was inspired, but still clueless about a Career in Tech.
My love for programming was spurred by the Google in Residence Program (GIR) in my University. I took an introductory CS class with a GIR Instructor, and I became convinced about my career path.
Technology inspires me everyday, and I always love the challenge!
